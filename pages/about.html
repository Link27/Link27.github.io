About Me


Once again, I am James Sastamoine! I am currently 28 years old and have spent most of my life here in Omaha, Nebraska.

I have been working with technology starting at a youngish age of 12 when I got my first iPhone Touch. I was very into the jailbreaking scene and also did a lot of self repairs and customization that really sparked my love for technology.

I studied Computer Science at the University of Nebraska, Omaha starting in August of 2012. Due to juggling a fulltime job on top of going to school, it took me a bit longer to earn my degree. But in May of 2020, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

Growing up baseball was one of my favorite sports and I played through most of my childhood. Now that I have grown up some more and clearly didn't become a professional baseball player, I haved moved onto fantasy baseball as one of my current hobbies. Going off of the sports theme, I also do love to play disc golf and also go bowling ever now and then. Still shooting for my first 300 game! (Current best is 289). From a technology standpoint for hobbies, I love to find mini projects to do whether it is on my computer or with smaller devices like cell phones or controllers. I still repair cell phones to this day whether it be for myself or close friends/family. I also built a custom NAS back in 2019 and host video game servers for my friends with custom scripts to help automate the process as much as possible.
