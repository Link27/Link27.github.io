About Me


I am 28 years old and have spent most of my life here in Omaha, Nebraska.

I have been working with technology starting at the age of 12 when I got my first iPhone Touch. I was very into the jailbreaking scene and also did a lot of self repairs and customization that sparked my love for technology.

I studied Computer Science at the University of Nebraska, Omaha starting in August of 2012. While attending school, I also had a fulltime job and was able to graduate in May of 2020 with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science.

In addition to my education at UNO, I also acquire new skills from personal projects. I am also looking at getting some certifications to continue to expand my knowledge and enhance my skills.

Growing up, baseball was one of my favorite sports and I played through most of my childhood. As an adult, one of my hobbies is now fantasy baseball.

I also enjoy playing disc golf and bowling. I bowled all four years on my high school's team and made varity in my Sophomore year. My current best score is 289. Still shooting for my first 300 game!

Other than sports, technology is one of my main forms of entertainment. I love to find mini projects to do, whether it is on the computer or with smaller devices, such as cell phones or controllers. I also built a custom NAS back in 2019 and host video game servers for my friends with custom scripts to help automate the process as much as possible.
